How to Play

Two teams take turns guessing the most popular survey answers. Type your answer and hit Submit. If it matches a hidden answer, the tile flips and your team earns those points. Three strikes and control passes to the other team. The team that reveals all answers — or has the most points after 8 rounds — wins!
